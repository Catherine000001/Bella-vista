Our Story

Crafted with
Passion & Soul

Born from a family kitchen in Florence, Bella Vista has grown into a celebrated institution of Italian fine dining.

In 1997, Chef Marco Pellegrini left the sun-drenched hills of Tuscany with a leather notebook of family recipes and an unshakeable belief: that a meal should feel like a homecoming. From those quiet beginnings, Bella Vista has evolved into a destination — earning two Michelin stars while never once forgetting its roots.

Our kitchen follows the ancient calendar. We source from a curated family of farmers, fishermen, and artisan producers — because seasonal ingredients speak with a clarity no technique can manufacture.

Our Promise

Why Bella Vista

Every detail has been considered so that your only task is to enjoy

🌿

Farm-to-Table Freshness

We partner directly with 18 certified organic farms across Tuscany and Emilia-Romagna, receiving deliveries six mornings a week. Your plate is never more than 36 hours from the earth.

👨‍🍳

Masters of Their Craft

Our brigade of 12 chefs averages 14 years of professional experience, trained across Florence, Lyon, and Tokyo. Every technique is deliberate; every garnish earns its place.

⚡

Seamless Service

From the moment you arrive until your last espresso, our floor team anticipates rather than reacts. Your evening is choreographed to feel effortless and entirely your own.

✨

An Atmosphere Apart

Housed in a restored 16th-century palazzo, Bella Vista pairs stone archways with candlelit intimacy. Our private dining rooms and terrace overlook the river for moments that ask to be remembered.
